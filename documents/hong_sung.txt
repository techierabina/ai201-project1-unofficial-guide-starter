The course expectations are always clear. No tests and typically does not meet at every lecture time,

learning at my own pace and basically only having to worry about the projects. His grading based on 
accuracy to his lecture code for the lessons was annoying, but once I got used to it wasn't really that bad. I enjoyed having no tests.
